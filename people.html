Lead Professor

Professor Rakesh Shiradkar

Research Director

Rakesh Shiradkar, Ph.D., is a healthcare informatics and data scientist specializing in AI-assisted technologies utilizing multi-modal medical data (imaging, genomics, and health records) to advance healthcare management. Formerly an assistant professor at Emory University and Georgia Tech, he completed postdoctoral training at Case Western Reserve University. Dr. Shiradkar leads a Mike Slive Foundation project on AI-based tools for predicting prostate cancer outcomes and has previously secured a $542,293 Department of Defense grant for noninvasive prostate cancer risk stratification using advanced imaging techniques.
