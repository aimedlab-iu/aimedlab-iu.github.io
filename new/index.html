Welcome to AIMED Lab

Revolutionizing healthcare through artificial intelligence and advanced medical imaging technologies.


Welcome to the AIMED Research Lab, where groundbreaking research at the convergence of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and medical imaging is shaping the future of healthcare. Our mission is to develop transformative technologies that enhance disease diagnosis, prognosis, and personalized care.

The Launch of AIMED Lab

The AIMED Research Lab, a pioneering hub at the forefront of medical imaging research, is redefining the future of healthcare. Combining the power of artificial intelligence, computer vision, and advanced computational imaging, the lab is developing groundbreaking solutions to enhance disease diagnosis, prognosis, and clinical decision-making. From leveraging imaging biomarkers for precision medicine to integrating multimodal health data for personalized care, AIMED is addressing some of the most pressing challenges in modern medicine. With a focus on fairness, reproducibility, and real-world applicability, their research is poised to make healthcare more accurate, equitable, and accessible.

Discover Our Vision

Unlock the potential of computational imaging to revolutionize healthcare. At the AIMED Lab, we are committed to driving innovation in:

  • AI-powered insights for early disease detection and predictive analytics.
  • Computational imaging biomarkers that enhance clinical precision.
  • Equity and reproducibility testing to ensure fairness and global applicability.
  • Integrative solutions combining multimodal health data for personalized medicine.
